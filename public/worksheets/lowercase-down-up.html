Worksheet 3.2: The "Down and Up" Letters

"These letters go down first, then up to make a bump: h, m, n, r, b, p"

Letter h (tall line + bump)

Letter m (line down + two bumps)

Letter n (line down + one bump)

Letter r (line down + small bump)

Letter b (tall line + bump at bottom)

Letter p (line down below + bump at top)

⬇️⬆️ Down and Up Tips:

  • • All letters start with a line down from the top
  • • h: Tall line + bump on the right side
  • • m: Short line + two bumps (like two n's together)
  • • n: Short line + one bump on the right
  • • r: Short line + small bump that doesn't go down
  • • b: Tall line + round bump at the bottom
  • • p: Line that goes below + round bump at the top
  • • The bump always curves up and over, then down
