⚡ Diagonal Tips:

  • • Keep diagonal lines at consistent angles
  • • A and V form triangular shapes
  • • M and W are like upside-down versions of each other
  • • X is two diagonal lines crossing in the middle
  • • Y combines diagonals with a vertical line
  • • Z uses one diagonal between two horizontal lines
